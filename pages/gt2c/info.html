Gran Turismo 2 Concept

About

Gran Turismo 2 Concept is a fan-made modification of the original Gran Turismo 2 game for the PlayStation. This project aims to enhance the gaming experience by redesigning the menus to resemble those of Gran Turismo 3, while also introducing new cars and events for players to enjoy.

Features

  • Redesigned menus inspired by Gran Turismo 3
  • New cars added to the roster
  • Additional racing events and challenges
